Topsoil Blends

Garden Compost

Ground Control Garden Compost is a 100% organic, greenwaste based compost approved for all types of gardening. Not all compost is equal. Ground Control Garden Compost is produced following stringent guidelines and lab tested for maturity, proper biological balance, and microbial activity.

Planting Soil

Tree-n-Shrub Planting Soil is an all-organic soil blend designed for growing shrubs, trees and other plants that grow best in a fungal dominated environment. Blended with the proper organic fertilizers, compost and beneficial microbes, you can create the ideal Soil Foodweb for your acid loving plants.

Lawn Base Soil

Premium Turf Soil is a sandy based soil that is biologically formulated for growing grass lawns. Turf Soil can be used for laying sod or seeding, or have Ground Control blow it in place with seed injected as it's being applied. Just add water for a beautiful lawn!

Topsoil

A clean topsoil, screened with no clumps. Easy to grade.

Veggie Soil

Ground Controls basic soilless mix, designed for vegetable gardens. Produced following stringent guidelines for proper biological balance and microbial activity. For gardeners that want to control their own fertilizer package.

Oregon Grown Soil

Ground Controls premium blend soilless mix for vegetable gardening. Complete with the beneficial microbe, compost and organic fertilizer, you can begin to build the proper soil food-web that best fits your plants.

Soilless container stock mix. Designed to allow good drainage, biologically active for optimal root growth.
